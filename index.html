HDPools - 0% IL, Profit Strategy Pools

By staking DAI token in a HDPool, you are simultaneously earning and providing buy pressure while enjoying minimal risk exposure and Impermanent Loss.HDPools provide a variety of profit strategy pools to earn interest. When you stake your tokens in the HDPools you will earn the deflationary asset $HDCORE.

The HDPools will utilise the interest earned to constantly buy HDCORE from the market. By staking in the HDPools you are providing buy pressure on the token you are earning.

Your earned HDCORE will be market ordered from your interest accrued and then claimed when you withdraw from the HDPool. Your total staked DAI balance represents the value of both your claimable HDCORE and DAI.
