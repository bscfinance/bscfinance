KVaults - Deflationary Farming

KVaults earn their APY from a 1.5% fee from all sell orders and transactions made with KORE. This includes all trading volume of DEXs and any transaction on the blockchain.

APY is calculated by the LP value; ETH price and KORE price and the trading volume - the more trading, the more profit KVault receives to share amongst the stakers.

$KORE Liquidity Generation Event

The KORE Liquidity Generation Event allows investors to contribute and lock their ETH. After the event is over, all contributed ETH will be deposited as liquidity to UniSwap.

ETH depositors will be able to claim their share of LP tokens once the event has ended and liquidity has been added.

By depositing, you are agreeing that the team behind KORE is not responsible for your funds after the event is over.
